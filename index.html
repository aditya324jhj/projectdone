Which vaccine is the best

There are basically three types of vaccine available for covid
1. TRADITIONAL VACCINE
Like COVAXIN
IN this type of vaccine dead or attenuated corona virus is injected in to your body.Dead means the virus which do not have capability to replicate.
2. RECOMBINANT DNA VACCINE.
EXAMPLE COVISHIELD OR SPUTNIK
In COVISHIELD in the DNA of chimpanzee ADENOVIRUS gene which have the capability to produce spike protein taken from genome of corona virus. Know this modified DNA of Adenovirus have capability to produce spike proteins just like corona virus. As our body makes antibodies against spike protein of corona virus.
3. LIPID NANO PARTICLE BASED VACCINE.
EXAMPLE PFIZER MODERNA
In this type of vaccine mRNA having information to make spike protein is packed in lipid particle.In this type of vaccine no virus is injected in to our body.Inside our cell mRNA uses our ribosome endoplasmic reticulum and Golgi apparatus to make spike protien against which antibodies are formed
